Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common inquiries about Liveenity's service and features.

What makes Liveenity different from other streaming services? +
Liveenity focuses on providing the most efficient and cost-effective video delivery solution for developers and businesses. Our platform is optimized for pre-recorded content streamed from various sources like cloud storage and URLs, offering superior performance and low latency.
Is Liveenity suitable for live streaming? +
While our core strength is on-demand, pre-recorded video delivery, we offer solutions that can integrate with existing live streaming infrastructure. Please contact our sales team for custom live streaming needs.
How do I get started with the API? +
You can find our full API documentation in the "API & Integrations" section above. Sign up for a free account to receive your API key and start integrating our service immediately.
What are the pricing plans? +
We offer several flexible pricing tiers, including a generous free plan, pay-as-you-go options, and custom enterprise solutions. You can view a detailed breakdown on our Pricing page linked in the header navigation.
